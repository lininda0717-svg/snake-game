How to Play

Controls

WASD: Move your snake (or drag the virtual joystick on touch devices).

Objective

Eat food to grow your snake. Your goal is to become the biggest snake in the arena!

Mechanics

  • The world is vast, filled with many AI snakes and abundant food.
  • Run into smaller snakes to eat them and absorb their length.
  • Avoid running into larger snakes, or you will be eaten.
  • Running into walls will end your game.
  • Your snake will not die if it runs into its own body.
  • Snakes with a length less than 50 have a shield (a blue aura) and cannot be eaten.
  • AI snakes will try to flee from larger snakes.
